How to use it?

01. It's gentle, and eye-safe

"Meticulously removes dirt"

02. Understand Your Skin Type

"Identify whether your skin is oily, dry, combination, or sensitive. Choose products specifically formulated for your skin type to avoid irritation."

03. Start with a Patch Test

" Before using a new product on your face, apply a small amount to a discreet area, like behind your ear, and wait 24-48 hours to check for any adverse reactions."

04. Follow a Simple Routine

" Use a gentle cleanser, moisturizer, and sunscreen daily. Introduce additional products like serums or exfoliants gradually, one at a time, to monitor how your skin reacts."

05. Use Products as Directed

"Follow the instructions on the label regarding application frequency and amount. Overusing or mixing certain products (e.g., retinol with acids) can cause skin damage."

06. Monitor for Changes

"If you notice any redness, irritation, or breakouts after starting a new product, stop using it immediately and consult a dermatologist if necessary."
